About The Mandalorian

The Mandalorian is set in the Star Wars universe, following a lone bounty hunter named Din Djarin in the outer reaches of the galaxy, far from the authority of the New Republic. After the fall of the Galactic Empire, Djarin is hired for a mysterious job that leads him to discover and protect a young alien known as Grogu (Baby Yoda), forging an unlikely father-son bond as they navigate a dangerous galaxy filled with remnants of the Empire and other threats.

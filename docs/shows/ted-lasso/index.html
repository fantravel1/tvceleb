About Ted Lasso

Ted Lasso follows an American college football coach who is unexpectedly recruited to manage a professional soccer team in England, AFC Richmond, despite having no experience with the sport. What begins as a publicity stunt by the team's new owner becomes a heartwarming story about the power of optimism, kindness, and believing in people. The series explores themes of leadership, mental health, and community through humor and genuine emotion.

? Frequently Asked Questions

Ted Lasso has 3 seasons with 34 episodes. It premiered on Apple TV+ on August 14, 2020 and concluded on May 31, 2023.

Ted Lasso is an Apple TV+ Original and is available exclusively on Apple TV+.

Ted Lasso originated as a character in NBC Sports promotional videos for their Premier League coverage. Jason Sudeikis played the character in these ads before developing it into a full series for Apple TV+.

While the show concluded after 3 seasons, Warner Bros. has been exploring the possibility of continuing the series, potentially with or without Jason Sudeikis. As of 2025, a fourth season has been announced.

Biography

Claudia Jessie is an English actress born on October 30, 1989, in Birmingham, England. She grew up in a bohemian household and was homeschooled before training in acting. She began her career in British television with small roles in shows like Doctors, WPC 56, and Vanity Fair before landing the role that would bring her global attention.

Jessie gained international recognition as Eloise Bridgerton, the outspoken, intellectually curious, and proto-feminist fifth Bridgerton sibling, in Netflix's Bridgerton (2020-present). Her performance as the society-averse Bridgerton who would rather read books and question gender roles than participate in the marriage market has made Eloise one of the most relatable and beloved characters in the series. The character's friendship and rivalry with Penelope Featherington has been a major ongoing storyline.

Before Bridgerton, Jessie appeared in the BBC supernatural drama series The Athena (2015) and the drama Line of Duty (2019). She has spoken about her unconventional upbringing, including living in a caravan as a child, and brings a grounded, naturalistic quality to her performances. She is known for her warmth and humor in interviews and has become a fan favorite within the Bridgerton community.

Biography

Simone Ashley, born Simone Ashwini Pillai, is a British actress of Indian Tamil descent, born on March 30, 1995, in London, England. She studied at the Arts Educational Schools in London and began her career with roles in British television including the Channel 4 series Broadchurch and the Netflix teen drama Sex Education.

Ashley achieved global recognition as Kate Sharma, the spirited and independent love interest of Anthony Bridgerton, in Season 2 of Netflix's Bridgerton (2022-present). Her compelling portrayal of the strong-willed eldest Sharma sister who matches wits with and eventually falls for the Viscount Bridgerton made her the romantic lead of the show's most-watched season. She became one of the most prominent South Asian actresses in mainstream Western television.

Before Bridgerton, Ashley appeared as Olivia Hanan in the Netflix series Sex Education (2019-2020) and had roles in various British productions. Following her Bridgerton success, she has been cast in prominent projects and has become a major figure in fashion, appearing on the covers of Vogue, Elle, and Harper's Bazaar. She has spoken publicly about the importance of representation for South Asian women in media.

Biography

Bryce Dallas Howard is an American actress and director born on March 2, 1981, in Los Angeles, California. The daughter of director Ron Howard, she studied at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts.

Howard has starred in major franchises including Jurassic World (2015-2022) and The Twilight Saga. She delivered a memorable performance as Lacie Pound in the Black Mirror episode "Nosedive" (2016), portraying a woman obsessed with social media ratings. She has also directed episodes of The Mandalorian and other Disney+ series.

Biography

Victor Dorobantu is a Romanian magician and actor born on August 29, 2002, in Bucharest, Romania. He developed a passion for magic and hand performance at a young age and became known for his exceptional manual dexterity and stage magic skills. His unique talents led to one of the most unusual and memorable casting decisions in recent television history.

Dorobantu became internationally famous for his role as Thing, the disembodied hand that serves as Wednesday Addams' loyal companion and sidekick, in Netflix's Wednesday (2022-present). Wearing a blue suit for CGI removal, Dorobantu performed all of Thing's movements with his actual hand, bringing remarkable personality and expressiveness to a character with no face or voice. His performance required extensive physical training and coordination, and the result was one of the show's most beloved characters.

Before Wednesday, Dorobantu was primarily known in Romania's magic community for his hand magic performances. He was discovered through his social media presence showcasing his remarkable hand skills and dexterity. The role of Thing catapulted him to international fame and demonstrated how specialized physical performance skills can create unforgettable screen characters.

Biography

Noah Cameron Schnapp is an American actor born on October 3, 2004, in New York City, New York. He grew up in Scarsdale, New York, and began acting at a young age, studying at the Westchester Broadway Theatre. His first major role was voicing Charlie Brown in the animated film The Peanuts Movie (2015).

Schnapp gained widespread recognition as Will Byers in the Netflix series Stranger Things (2016-2025). As the boy whose disappearance into the Upside Down sets the entire series in motion, Schnapp's emotional performance anchored the show's central mystery. In later seasons, his portrayal of Will's struggles with the lasting effects of his time in the Upside Down and his personal identity earned particular critical praise.

Beyond Stranger Things, Schnapp has appeared in the films Bridge of Spies (2015) directed by Steven Spielberg, We Only Know So Much (2018), and Abe (2019). He launched a sustainable food company called TBH (To Be Honest) in 2022, producing hazelnut spreads with reduced sugar. Schnapp came out publicly as gay in early 2023, receiving widespread support. He has been a vocal advocate for LGBTQ+ youth.

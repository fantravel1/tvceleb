Biography

Hayley Elizabeth Atwell is a British-American actress born on April 5, 1982, in London, England. She trained at the Guildhall School of Music and Drama and built a career in theater, film, and television.

Atwell is best known for playing Peggy Carter in the MCU, appearing in Captain America films and the ABC series Agent Carter (2015-2016). She starred in the Black Mirror episode "Be Right Back" (2013) as Martha, delivering an emotional performance about grief and technology.

Biography

Joy Sunday is an American actress born on September 8, 1996, in Indianapolis, Indiana. She is of Nigerian descent and grew up in a family that emphasized education and the arts. She studied acting and graduated from Northwestern University before moving to Los Angeles to pursue her career in the entertainment industry.

Sunday gained significant recognition as Bianca Barclay, the queen bee siren at Nevermore Academy and Wednesday Addams' rival, in Netflix's Wednesday (2022-present). Her commanding performance as the confident, vocally gifted student who initially clashes with Wednesday before developing a more complex relationship earned her praise for bringing depth and nuance to what could have been a one-dimensional antagonist.

Before Wednesday, Sunday appeared in the television series Dear White People on Netflix and in various short films and independent projects. She has also worked in theater and is trained in multiple performance disciplines. With the success of Wednesday, Sunday has established herself as a rising talent in Hollywood and has spoken about the importance of diverse representation in genre television.

Biography

Luke George Newton is an English actor born on February 5, 1993, in Brighton, England. He trained in performing arts from a young age and began his career in British television. He appeared as Luke Morgan in the long-running soap opera Hollyoaks and built a steady career in British entertainment before his international breakthrough.

Newton gained global recognition as Colin Bridgerton, the charming third Bridgerton son, in Netflix's Bridgerton (2020-present). While Colin appeared throughout the first two seasons, Newton took center stage in Season 3, which focused on Colin's love story with Penelope Featherington (Nicola Coughlan). His transformation from oblivious gentleman to passionate romantic lead captivated audiences worldwide and established him as a major leading man.

Before Bridgerton, Newton appeared in the British soap opera The Lodge (2016-2017) on Disney Channel and Hollyoaks. He studied at the London School of Musical Theatre and has a background in singing and dance as well as acting. Following his Bridgerton success, Newton has become a prominent figure in romantic drama and has expanded his career into film projects.

Biography

Storm Reid is an American actress born on July 1, 2003, in Atlanta, Georgia. She began acting at a very young age, appearing in the film 12 Years a Slave (2013) when she was just nine years old. She went on to build a remarkable career as a child and young adult actress in both film and television.

Reid gained significant recognition for her role as Riley Abel, Ellie's best friend, in the HBO series The Last of Us (2023-present). Her performance in the emotionally devastating episode "Left Behind" (Season 1, Episode 7) was widely praised for its tenderness and depth, portraying the deep bond between Riley and Ellie in the early days of the outbreak. The episode is considered one of the strongest of the first season.

Beyond The Last of Us, Reid starred as Meg Murry in Ava DuVernay's A Wrinkle in Time (2018), one of the few big-budget films to feature a young Black female lead. She appeared in The Invisible Man (2020), The Suicide Squad (2021), and the television series Euphoria (2019-2022) as Gia Bennett, Rue's younger sister. Reid is also a student at the University of Southern California and has been recognized for her academic achievements alongside her acting career.

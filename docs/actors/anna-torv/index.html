Biography

Anna Torv is an Australian actress born on June 15, 1979, in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. She is the niece of media mogul Rupert Murdoch and studied at Australia's National Institute of Dramatic Art (NIDA). She built her early career in Australian television before achieving international success.

Torv first gained major recognition as Olivia Dunham, the FBI agent investigating fringe science cases, in the Fox series Fringe (2008-2013), created by J.J. Abrams. She later delivered a critically acclaimed performance as Tess Servopoulos, the tough FEDRA officer and Joel's smuggling partner, in the HBO series The Last of Us (2023-present). Her portrayal of Tess's hardened exterior masking deep emotion in the show's early episodes earned widespread praise.

Beyond these roles, Torv appeared as Wendy Carr in the Netflix series Mindhunter (2017-2019), David Fincher's critically acclaimed series about FBI profilers studying serial killers. She has also appeared in the Australian series The Secret Life of Us (2003-2005) and various Australian films. Torv is known for her ability to bring intelligence and emotional complexity to every role she takes on.

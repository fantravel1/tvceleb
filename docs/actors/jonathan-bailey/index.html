Biography

Jonathan Stuart Bailey is an English actor born on April 25, 1988, in Wallingford, Oxfordshire, England. He began acting as a child, appearing in productions at the Royal Shakespeare Company and the National Theatre. He trained at the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama and built an impressive career spanning stage and screen from a young age.

Bailey achieved global fame as Anthony Bridgerton, the eldest Bridgerton sibling and Viscount, in the Netflix series Bridgerton (2020-present). His smoldering performance as the duty-bound eldest brother navigating love and family obligations, particularly his enemies-to-lovers romance with Kate Sharma (Simone Ashley) in Season 2, made him one of the most popular romantic leads on television. The role introduced him to a massive worldwide audience.

Before Bridgerton, Bailey was a celebrated West End performer, winning the Laurence Olivier Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role in a Musical for his performance in the revival of Company (2019). He has also appeared in the television series Broadchurch (2013), Crashing (2016), and W1A (2014-2017). He starred alongside Ariana Grande in the film Wicked (2024) as Fiyero. Bailey is openly gay and has been an advocate for LGBTQ+ representation in the arts.

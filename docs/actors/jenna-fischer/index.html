Biography

Regina Marie Fischer, known professionally as Jenna Fischer, is an American actress born on March 7, 1974, in Fort Wayne, Indiana. She grew up in St. Louis, Missouri, and studied theater at Truman State University. She moved to Los Angeles in 1998 and spent years in small roles and independent films before her breakthrough.

Fischer is best known for her role as Pam Beesly, the warm, artistic receptionist-turned-saleswoman at Dunder Mifflin, in NBC's The Office (2005-2013). Her understated, naturalistic performance and her on-screen romance with Jim Halpert (John Krasinski) became the emotional heart of the series. The Jim-Pam love story is widely considered one of the greatest romances in television history, and Fischer's portrayal earned her an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Comedy Series.

Beyond The Office, Fischer has appeared in films including Blades of Glory (2007), Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story (2007), and The Giant Mechanical Man (2012). She starred in the ABC comedy Splitting Up Together (2018-2019) and co-hosts the popular podcast Office Ladies with her real-life best friend and former co-star Angela Kinsey, offering a behind-the-scenes look at The Office. She authored the book The Actor's Life: A Survival Guide (2017).

Biography

Hunter Doohan is an American actor born on January 19, 1994, in Fort Smith, Arkansas. He grew up in Arkansas and moved to Los Angeles to pursue acting, studying at Oklahoma City University. He built his early career through guest roles on television shows including Truth Be Told and Your Honor before his major breakthrough.

Doohan gained widespread recognition as Tyler Galpin, the charming barista and love interest with a dark secret, in Netflix's Wednesday (2022-present). His layered performance as the seemingly sweet small-town boy who harbors a sinister connection to the show's central mystery earned him praise for his ability to shift between warmth and menace. The role significantly raised his profile in Hollywood.

Before Wednesday, Doohan appeared in the Showtime series Your Honor (2020-2023) alongside Bryan Cranston, playing Adam Desiato, the son of a judge entangled in a moral crisis. He also appeared in the film Schooled (2019) and had guest appearances in various television series. Doohan is openly gay and married producer Fielder Jewett in 2022.

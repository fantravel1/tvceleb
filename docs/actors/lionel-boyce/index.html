Biography

Lionel Boyce is an American actor, writer, and voice actor born on September 2, 1990, in Los Angeles, California. He is a longtime member of the Odd Future collective, the creative group founded by Tyler, the Creator, and began his career in entertainment through that connection, contributing writing and voice work to various Odd Future projects.

Boyce gained his most prominent role as Marcus Brooks, the quiet, dedicated pastry chef at "The Beef" who discovers a passion for fine dessert-making, in the FX series The Bear (2022-present). His understated yet deeply moving performance, particularly in storylines involving Marcus's artistic growth and personal losses, has earned widespread praise from critics and audiences. Marcus's journey of self-discovery through culinary art has resonated with viewers as one of the show's most touching arcs.

Before The Bear, Boyce was best known for his voice acting work as the voice of various characters on the Adult Swim animated series Loiter Squad (2012-2014) and The Jellies (2015-2019), which he also co-created with Tyler, the Creator. He has written for several television projects and continues to balance acting with his behind-the-scenes creative work.

Biography

Emma Myers is an American actress born on April 2, 2002, in Orlando, Florida. She was homeschooled and began acting at a young age, taking on small roles in independent films and short films. Her career trajectory changed dramatically with a single breakout role that introduced her to a global audience.

Myers gained international recognition as Enid Sinclair, Wednesday Addams' bubbly, colorful werewolf roommate, in Netflix's Wednesday (2022-present). Her warm, energetic performance provided the perfect counterbalance to Jenna Ortega's deadpan Wednesday, and the dynamic between the two characters became one of the show's most beloved elements. The role made Myers an instant fan favorite and launched her into the spotlight.

Following her breakthrough in Wednesday, Myers has continued to build her career with notable projects. She starred in the Netflix film A Good Girl's Guide to Murder (2024) based on the bestselling novel, and in the coming-of-age film Family Switch (2023) alongside Jennifer Garner. She is set to continue her role as Enid in future seasons of Wednesday. Myers is known for her approachable personality on social media and her growing fanbase.

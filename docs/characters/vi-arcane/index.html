Vi is a fierce, hot-headed fighter from the undercity of Zaun who serves as a protective older sister to Powder. A natural leader with her father Vander's sense of justice, Vi channels her anger at Piltover's oppression into action ‚Äî sometimes reckless action. Her sister Powder has become Jinx, Silco controls the undercity, and the tensions between Zaun and Piltover have only deepened. Vi's quest to reconnect with her sister while navigating this new landscape drives the show's emotional narrative.

Character Arc

Bianca Barclay is Nevermore Academy's reigning queen ‚Äî a siren with the ability to use her voice to manipulate and persuade others. Beautiful, talented, and fiercely competitive, she initially serves as Wednesday's primary social antagonist, viewing the new student as a threat to her established dominance at the school.

Beneath Bianca's confident exterior lies deep insecurity rooted in her siren nature. She can never be certain whether people genuinely like her or are simply under the influence of her powers. Her relationship with Xavier Thorpe is haunted by this doubt, and her rivalry with Wednesday is fueled partly by envy of someone who is authentically, unapologetically themselves without any supernatural persuasion.

Bianca's backstory reveals that her mother leads a siren-based cult called the Morning Song, and Bianca fled that world to attend Nevermore. The threat of being pulled back into the cult adds genuine stakes to her character, transforming her from a superficial mean-girl archetype into someone fighting for autonomy over her own identity.

As the season progresses, Bianca and Wednesday develop a grudging mutual respect. When Nevermore is threatened, Bianca stands alongside Wednesday rather than against her, proving that her strength extends beyond her siren abilities.

Trivia & Fun Facts

  • The rivalry between Bianca and Wednesday was modeled after classic teen movie dynamics but with a supernatural twist.

? Frequently Asked Questions

Joy Sunday plays Bianca Barclay in the Netflix series Wednesday. Sunday is an actress and singer known for her roles in independent films before landing the breakout role at Nevermore Academy.

Bianca is a siren, meaning she has the supernatural ability to use her voice to manipulate and persuade others. This power is both her greatest strength and the source of her deepest insecurity.

The Morning Song is a cult led by Bianca's mother, Gabrielle. It operates as a siren-based organization that exploits people using siren powers. Bianca escaped the cult to attend Nevermore Academy.

Bianca and Wednesday start as rivals at Nevermore Academy, but their relationship evolves over the season. By the finale they fight on the same side to defend the school, developing a grudging mutual respect.

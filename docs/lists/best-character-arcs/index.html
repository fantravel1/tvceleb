Walter White's transformation from mild-mannered chemistry teacher to ruthless drug lord Heisenberg in Breaking Bad is widely considered the greatest character arc in TV history. Other standout arcs include Jaime Lannister in Game of Thrones, Jamie Tartt in Ted Lasso, and Jesse Pinkman in Breaking Bad.

A great character arc involves meaningful, believable change driven by the character's experiences and choices. The best arcs feel both surprising and inevitable, with each step of the transformation feeling earned and organic to the story.
